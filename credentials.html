I'm Emre, a self taught mobile developer. I have been working on Android development in different industries for a long time.

When I was telling people I was an Android developer, people would immediately ask "What about iOS?". iOS was not a requirement for my jobs, but I was feeling I needed to work on that platform as well.

Now, here I am, developing Android and iOS apps for corporations, entrepreneurs, SMEs and family members.
